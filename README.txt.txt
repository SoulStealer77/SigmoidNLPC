This is an chatbot that can mentain a normal conversation with user, also as request he can tell you weather in any city.
He can detect from conversation your emotional state and recomand an local from Chisinau to increase or decrease emotions
depending on how you feel at the moment.

Bot discord URL for invite: 
https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=960498317488431135&permissions=0&scope=bot

1)Download all files
2)Open them in any python IDE
3)Make sure you're logged on to the Discord website.
4)Copy bot URL, paste the URL intro your browser, choose a server to invite the bot to, and click Authorize.
5)Main bot file is corebot.py, launch this one, and bot will be activated in your discord server, where you addem him.
